# Design Business Terms for Copilot in Dynamics 365 Apps

## Overview

Business terms in Dynamics 365 Copilot help the AI understand organization-specific terminology, ensuring accurate and contextual responses.

## What Are Business Terms?

Business terms are custom vocabulary definitions that help Copilot understand:
- Industry-specific jargon
- Company-specific acronyms
- Product names and SKUs
- Process terminology
- Regional terminology

## Configuring Business Terms

### In Dynamics 365 Sales
- Define sales-specific terminology
- Product and service names
- Competitor names
- Sales process stages
- Territory definitions

### In Dynamics 365 Customer Service
- Case categories
- Product/service terminology
- Support tiers
- SLA definitions
- Knowledge article categories

## Best Practices

1. **Be Specific**: Clear, unambiguous definitions
2. **Include Context**: When and how terms are used
3. **Provide Examples**: Sample usage
4. **Regular Updates**: Keep terminology current
5. **Avoid Overlap**: No conflicting definitions

## Example Business Terms

```
Term: "Enterprise Plus"
Definition: Our premium service tier including 24/7 support, dedicated account manager, and custom integrations
Context: Used in sales and support scenarios
Synonyms: EP, Enterprise+

Term: "NRR"
Definition: Net Revenue Retention - measures revenue growth from existing customers
Context: Sales metrics and forecasting
Formula: (Starting MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting MRR
```

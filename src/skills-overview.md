# Skills at a Glance

The AB-100 exam measures your ability to accomplish the following technical tasks:

## Exam Weight Distribution

* **Plan AI-powered business solutions (25–30%)**
* **Design AI-powered business solutions (25–30%)**
* **Deploy AI-powered business solutions (40–45%)**

## What This Means

The exam places the greatest emphasis on deployment (40-45%), followed by equal weight on planning and design (25-30% each). This distribution reflects the practical, implementation-focused nature of the solution architect role.

### Plan AI-powered Business Solutions

This section tests your ability to:
- Analyze requirements for AI-powered business solutions
- Design overall AI strategy for business solutions
- Evaluate the costs and benefits of an AI-powered business solution

### Design AI-powered Business Solutions

This section tests your ability to:
- Design AI and agents for business solutions
- Design extensibility of AI solutions
- Orchestrate configuration for prebuilt agents and apps

### Deploy AI-powered Business Solutions

This section tests your ability to:
- Analyze, monitor, and tune AI-powered business solutions
- Manage the testing of AI-powered business solutions
- Design the ALM process for AI-powered business solutions
- Design responsible AI, security, governance, risk management, and compliance

## Study Strategy

Given the weight distribution, you should:

1. **Allocate Study Time Proportionally**: Spend the most time on deployment topics (40-45% of your study time).
2. **Focus on Hands-On Practice**: Especially for deployment, testing, and monitoring scenarios.
3. **Understand End-to-End Scenarios**: Know how planning, design, and deployment work together.
4. **Master the Microsoft Stack**: Deep knowledge of Copilot Studio, Azure AI Foundry, Dynamics 365, and Power Platform is essential.
